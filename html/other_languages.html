Hugging Face

Hugging face is like a library of many AI models. It has models that perform specific tasks very well. They are like specialized ChatGPT models. Sometimes, people want a specific AI model for a certain task, that is when they would want to use an AI from Hugging Face. You could even make an account for free to use some of the AIs.

TensorFlow

TensorFlow is mainly used for machine learning and using what are known as tensors. Tensors are like vectors of information, whiceh machine attach values to to evaluate patterns in data inputs.

SQL

Personally, I like to have a block of code to change easily with the click of my mouse or touchpad. This is just not for everyone. Though I can see why a company may want data to be harder to change. This could mean that more thought is put into the value of the variables and things have to be more intentional. It kind of reminds me of excel with how often I see tables being made with it.
